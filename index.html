Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this thrilling action game!

About The Game

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must fend off endless waves of monsters. Upgrade your character, unlock new abilities, and see how long you can survive in this challenging environment.

The game features intuitive controls, stunning visuals, and an engaging progression system that will keep you coming back for more!

Game Features

Endless Gameplay

Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters as you progress through the game. How long can you survive?

Character Progression

Collect experience and upgrade your character with new abilities and enhanced stats.

Diverse Enemies

Battle against a wide variety of monsters, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns.

Power-ups & Weapons

Discover and collect different weapons and power-ups to enhance your survival chances.

How To Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map
  • Attacks are automatic - position yourself strategically to maximize damage
  • Collect experience gems dropped by defeated monsters
  • Level up to choose new abilities and upgrades
  • Survive as long as possible and aim for a high score!

Tips & Strategies

  • Focus on movement-based upgrades early to help avoid enemies
  • Don't stay in one place for too long - keep moving to avoid being surrounded
  • Prioritize upgrades that complement your playstyle
  • Try to funnel enemies through narrow passages to deal with them more efficiently
  • Balance offensive and defensive abilities for the best survival chance
